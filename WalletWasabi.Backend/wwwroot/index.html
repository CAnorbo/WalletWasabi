Better money, Better world

Satoshi brought us scarcity, Wasabi is bringing us fungibility. As inflation is running rampant, vigilant individuals are opting out. Bitcoin is rapidly becoming the world's reserve currency, but its fungibility - a property of good money - is still unsatisfactory. Wasabi Wallet is a bastion of hope in the quest to qualitatively improve the money of our future.

Not your keys, not your coins

If you don't control your private keys, then you are not the true owner of the funds. Wasabi is non-custodial: the developers cannot steal or freeze your funds.

Always be coinjoining

Wasabi protects your financial information through coinjoins, client side block filtering, and communicating over the Tor anonymity network. Neither the public nor the developers can breach your privacy.

Don't trust verify

In order to ensure the code - that's running on your computer - can indeed protect you from the developers and from the public, Wasabi is free, open-source and deterministically reproducible software.
