Why Wasabi is the standard for Bitcoin privacy

  • Open Source

    Wasabi is free, open-source and deterministically reproducible software. Anyone can see, verify and even contribute to the code, demonstrating that there is nothing to hide.

  • Trustless by Design

    The software is designed so that neither the public nor the developers can breach your privacy. This is done through coinjoins, client-side block filtering, and communication over the Tor anonymity network.

  • Non-custodial

    Not your keys, not your coins. Wasabi Wallet lets you control your private keys, offering you true financial self sovereignty.

  • Automatic CoinJoin

    The wallet does all the work for you. You just have to choose how private you want your Bitcoin life to be.

Bringing Privacy to the Masses

Coinjoin Any Amount

Traditional coinjoins require users to have UTXO's of certain amounts and their privacy is based on equal value outputs anonymity set. With WabiSabi's' unequal amount coinjoins, there's no minimun amount required nor is the privacy solely based on anonymity set.

Unlimited Free Remixes

Whenever you send a payment with freshly coinjoined coins, you and the recipient are able to coinjoin those coins without paying any coordinator fees*. Remixes are also free* as well as coinjoinin UTXOs smaller than less 1 million satoshi.

Easy to use UI

Wasabi is designed to be a user friendly and intuitive wallet. User doesn't need to worry about coin selection, coinjoining or change avoidance. The software handles everything automatically by default.

*Bitcoin network fees still apply.
