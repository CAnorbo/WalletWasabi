The wallet

We don’t, we haven’t and we never will collect information from you. We live in a world where your information is being used to typecast, manipulate and surveil you. What you do with your Bitcoin is your business, so use Wasabi Wallet to retain your individuality, regain your identity and ultimately, reclaim your privacy. Why Wasabi

The BTC ecosystem is totally transparent, which is a great feature since transparency makes BTC valid. But there is no perfect system, thus, this system has errors and drawbacks as well. One of the drawbacks of this system is a lack of individual privacy.

This issue may lead to losing your money. How? Just imagine broadcasting all your information to the world. You wouldn’t want that. You don’t want to show your finances and transactions to everyone.

Coinjoin

CoinJoin is a Bitcoin transaction where multiple users combine their UTXO (Unspent Transaction Outputs) into one large transaction with multiple inputs and multiple outputs. A traditional Bitcoin transaction is usually composed of one sender and one recipient. It is easy to understand, even by an external observer, which inputs correspond to which outputs and vice versa. The purpose of a CoinJoin transaction composed by multiple inputs and outputs is to break blockchain surveillance heuristics.
