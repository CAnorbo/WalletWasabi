Why Wasabi Wallet?

We believe that privacy is the biggest problem in Bitcoin today. Wasabi Wallet was created to help Bitcoin maintain it's cypherpunk roots in it's path to global reserve currency and to fix some of the inherent privacy issues of an open and public ledger.

According to our research, having good privacy tools is not enough - If they are not easy to use, you would stand out in the crowd. It's also clear that most users don't bother to do extensive research on how to preserve their privacy and use the tools correctly. This is why Wasabi is designed to provide privacy by default, making it easy to use for as many people as possible.

Privacy for everyone

Traditional coinjoins require users to have a certain amount, because their privacy is based on equal value outputs. Usually this minimum required amount is fairly high, making it impossible for many to coinjoin. We did a long research and found out it's actually possible to provide privacy even with unequal amounts, hence we came up with new WabiSabi-powered arbitrary amount coinjoins, in which there's no minimum amount required, making bitcoin privacy more accessible for everyone.

Easy to use UI

Wasabi is designed to be a user friendly and intuitive Bitcoin wallet. User doesn't need to worry about coin selection, coinjoining or change avoidance. The software handles everything automatically by default.

Unlimited Free Remixes

To be able to scale coinjoin-privacy we need round-interoperability, hence we decided to insentivise remixing by making it free* even after one hop. So whenever user sends a payment with freshly coinjoined coins, sender and the recipient are able to coinjoin those coins without paying any coordinator fees*. Coinjoining UTXOs smaller than 1 million satoshi is also free*.

*Bitcoin network fees still apply.

Why Wasabi is the standard for Bitcoin privacy

  • Open Source

    Wasabi is free, open-source and deterministically reproducible software. Anyone can see, verify and even contribute to the code, demonstrating that there is nothing to hide.

  • Trustless by Design

    The software is designed so that neither the public nor the developers can breach your privacy. This is done through coinjoins, client-side block filtering, and communication over the Tor anonymity network.

  • Non-custodial

    Not your keys, not your coins. Wasabi Wallet lets you control your private keys, offering you true financial self sovereignty.

  • Automatic CoinJoin

    The wallet does all the work for you. You just have to choose how private you want your Bitcoin life to be.
